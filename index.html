About

My name is Brendan, and I'm from Portland. I believe that empathy should inform not just the way we design products, but the way we collaborate.

Before UX, I worked as a barista in Portland's thriving specialty coffee industry for a decade. It was in that space that I learned how to work with a team to craft a high-quality product in a fast-paced, customer-facing environment. Always having been a musician and creative person in general, I naturally enjoy tinkering endlessly in pursuit of giving people a positive emotional experience, whether that's with the perfect cup of coffee or a song that they connect with.

After some soul-searching that took me to LA for a music business intership and back to Portland for a code bootcamp, I realized that I am a designer at heart, and set out to solve the challenge of synthesizing all my experiences and passions into a new path.

Bringing the same passion for creativity, critical thining and empathy I developed as a musican and barista, I found my niche as a UX designer

Now I focus on extending the concept of empathy beyond product and into collaboration, and am passionate about improving the way product designers, visual designers and developers collaborate through design systems.
