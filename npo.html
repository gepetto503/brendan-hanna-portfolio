Non Profit Donator

Company: Epicodus Capstone Project

Project: Invision prototype of an app for managing recurring donations.

Role:UX design

Tech:Sketch 3, Invision

The vision for this app is to have a streamlined experience where the user can easily customize and automate donations to Non Profit Organizations, as well as curate which organizations they see content from.

The app will include an interactive map to search NPOs, the ability to invite NPOs to join the platform and thus be able to receive automated donations, and a dashboard where they can manage recurring or one-time donations. Stretch goals include a social media micro-platform where users can interact with organizations and other users.
